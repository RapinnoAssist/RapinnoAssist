Ticketing System in Cogniassist

A ticketing system is a valuable tool that simplifies the process of reporting, managing, and resolving issues, questions, or requests. It provides an organized approach for handling user inquiries effectively.

Key Features:​

  1. Report Issues and Questions

    • Users can submit detailed descriptions of their problems or questions.
    • The system creates a unique "ticket" for each submission, making tracking easy and efficient.

  2. Monitor Progress

    • Each ticket lets users and support teams follow the current status, receive updates, and review resolution steps as they occur.

  3. Engage in Direct Communication

    • Users and support staff can communicate through the ticket, ensuring clarity and speeding up the problem-solving process.

  4. Prioritize and Organize Tickets

    • Tickets are organized by priority, urgency, or category, ensuring that the most critical issues are addressed promptly.

Advantages​

A ticketing system boosts efficiency, maintains organization, and enhances the user experience by providing a clear and structured method for addressing concerns and tracking resolutions.

Step-by-Step Guide to Sending and Tracking Tickets

Provide the necessary details in the following fields to add the tickets:

Ticket Creation​

  1. Fill Out the Ticket Form:
    • Title: Provide a brief, descriptive title for your issue.
    • Assigned To: Select the person or team responsible for handling the issue.
    • Priority: Choose the urgency level (e.g., Low, Medium, High).
    • Upload File: Attach any relevant files, screenshots, or documents that could help in resolving the issue.
    • Describe the Issue: Provide a detailed description, including any steps to reproduce the problem, expected outcomes, and actual results.
  2. Create the Ticket: Once all fields are completed, click on createthe ticket to create a record of your issue.

Email is send​
